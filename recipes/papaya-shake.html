Ingredients

  • 1 cup fresh ripe papaya, peeled and cubed (approx. 1/2 a medium papaya).
  • 1/2 cup rolled oats (complex carbohydrates for sustained fuel).
  • 2 tablespoons creamy peanut butter(for healthy fats and natural protein).
  • 1 1/2 cups whole milk (or soy milk for a high-protein dairy-free option).
  • 1 teaspoon chia seeds (optional, for extra Omega-3 and fiber).
  • Ice cubes (optional, for a chilled, smoothie-like consistency).

Steps

  1. Mill the oats: For an extra-smooth finish, blend the dry oats first until they reach a fine, flour-like consistency.
  2. Add the fruit and fats: Place the fresh papaya cubes and the two generous tablespoons of peanut butter into the blender.
  3. Combine liquids: Pour in the milk and add the chia seeds (if using) along with the ice cubes.
  4. Blend: Process on high speed for 60 to 90 seconds until the mixture is completely velvety and homogeneous.
  5. Serve: Pour into a tall glass. You can garnish with a few small papaya chunks on top for a professional look.
