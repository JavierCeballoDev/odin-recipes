By combining the fast-absorbing energy of ripe bananas with the sustained release of complex carbohydrates from oats, this shake serves as a perfect post-workout recovery fuel. Ingredients

  • 2 ripe bananas.
  • 1/2 cup rolled oats.
  • 2 tablespoons creamy peanut butter.
  • 1 1/2 cups whole milk (or soy milk).
  • A pinch of ground cinnamon.
  • Ice cubes (optional).

Steps

  1. Prep the oats: Blend the dry oats first for a smoother, flour-like consistency.
  2. Add the base: Add the banana chunks and peanut butter to the blender.
  3. Combine liquids and spice: Pour in the milk and add the cinnamon and ice.
  4. Blend: Process on high speed for 45 to 60 seconds until completely creamy.
  5. Serve: Pour into a tall glass and enjoy your natural fuel.
